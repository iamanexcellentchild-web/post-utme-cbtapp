About UNILAG CBT Practice

Our Mission

UNILAG CBT Practice is designed to help students prepare effectively for the University of Lagos Post-UTME Computer Based Tests. We provide a comprehensive platform for students to practice, learn, and excel in their examinations.

Features

  • 📚 Comprehensive question bank covering all UNILAG subjects
  • ⏱️ Realistic timed exams matching actual exam conditions
  • 📊 Detailed analytics and performance tracking
  • ✅ Instant feedback with explanations
  • 🎯 Performance history and progress monitoring
  • 💡 Expert-curated content

How It Works

  1. Create your account
  2. Browse available exams
  3. Take practice tests under timed conditions
  4. Review detailed results and explanations
  5. Track your progress over time
  6. Master the concepts and ace your exams
